Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 3, 2025

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The Khmer Calendar add-on runs entirely within your Google Sheet. All date calculations happen in real-time on your device. No information leaves your Google account.

2. Permissions Explained

The add-on requests the following permission:

  • See, edit, create, and delete your spreadsheets in Google Sheets
    → Needed only to insert the lunar date into the selected cell.

We do not read your emails, files, or any other data.

3. Third-Party Services

This add-on uses:

  • Google Apps Script – to run the code
  • Google Sheets API – to write the result

No external servers or analytics are used.

4. Children's Privacy

This add-on is safe for all ages. No personal information is ever collected.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with a new "Effective Date".
